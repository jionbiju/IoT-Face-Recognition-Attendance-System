Tips:
  • DroidCam not showing? Make sure DroidCam app is running on both phone and computer
  • Poor quality? Check your WiFi connection and DroidCam video quality settings
  • Camera not working? Try restarting DroidCam on both devices
  • Multiple cameras? Higher resolution cameras are usually DroidCam
